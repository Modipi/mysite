Welcome to Livestock Haven ,founded by Modipi Boitumelo, where tradition meets excellence in every product we create. Since 2009, we’ve been passionately dedicated to providing our customers with the finest dairy products,

the juiciest steaks, and top-quality leather goods.
Nestled in the heart of nature, we pride ourselves on ethical farming practices that ensure the health and well-being of our livestock, while delivering only the best to your table and beyond.



Our commitment to quality is unwavering, from the care we give our animals to the craftsmanship that goes into every product.

Whether you're enjoying a rich glass of fresh milk, savoring a tender cut of steak, exploring our luxurious leather goods, you can trust that each item from Livestock Haven has been carefully crafted with love, respect, and the highest standards in mind.



At Livestock Haven, it's more than just farming – it's about creating a legacy of exceptional products that you can feel good about. Join us in celebrating the true taste of quality, the way nature intended.
